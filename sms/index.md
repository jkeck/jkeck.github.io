## What This Is

This is a private home automation project. I operate a personal SMS notification system that sends daily briefings and event reminders to a small, explicitly opted-in group of recipients (currently two people: myself and one other person). No marketing, no promotions, no third parties.

## How Recipients Opt In

SMS notifications from this system are sent only to recipients who have been directly and personally invited by me (Jessie Keck) and have given explicit verbal or written consent to receive them.

No one is added to this system without first being asked directly and confirming they wish to receive messages. There is no public sign-up form because this is a private, invitation-only system with a fixed recipient list.

Message frequency: Up to 2–3 messages per day (morning briefing, occasional event reminders). Standard message and data rates may apply.

## How to Opt Out

Reply with any of the following keywords and you will receive no further messages: **STOP**, **UNSUBSCRIBE**, **CANCEL**, **END**, **QUIT**

You may also contact me directly at [{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }}) to be removed.

To opt back in after opting out, reply **START** or contact me directly.

## Help

Reply **HELP** at any time for information about this service, or contact [{{ site.email }}]({{ site.email }}).

## Privacy Policy

**Information collected:** Phone numbers of opted-in recipients are stored locally on a private device. No other personal information is collected from recipients through this system.

**How information is used:** Phone numbers are used solely to deliver SMS notifications to opted-in recipients. They are not used for any other purpose.

**Information sharing:** No mobile information or phone numbers are shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers are not sold, rented, or shared with any outside parties. SMS opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third party.

**Service providers:** This system uses Twilio to transmit SMS messages. Twilio may process phone numbers as necessary to deliver messages.

**Data retention:** Phone numbers are retained only as long as a recipient remains opted in. Upon opt-out, the number is removed from the active recipient list.

**Contact:** Questions or removal requests: [{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }})
